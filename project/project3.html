The final movie I will be talking about is the movie The call, a 2020 South Korean thriller film directed by Lee Chung-hyun. The movie is about the main character buy a house where a girl, oh-young-sook met her tragic end. The main character is able to get into contact with the oh-young-sook from the past and both work together to rewrite eachothers history, untill Oh-young-sook finds out she has the ultimate power and starts to blackmail the main character to rewrite her history in selfish ways.
